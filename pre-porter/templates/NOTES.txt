3. To check the status of image pre-pulling DaemonSets:

   kubectl get daemonsets -l app.kubernetes.io/instance={{ .Release.Name }}

4. To view the logs of model cache containers:

   kubectl logs -l app.kubernetes.io/instance={{ .Release.Name }} -c model-cache

5. To check which nodes have successfully pulled images:

   kubectl get pods -l app.kubernetes.io/instance={{ .Release.Name }} -o wide

6. To refresh/re-pull all images, restart the DaemonSets:

   kubectl rollout restart daemonset -l app.kubernetes.io/instance={{ .Release.Name }}

7. To enable/disable specific images, update your values.yaml and run:

   helm upgrade {{ .Release.Name }} pre-porter -f your-values.yaml

8. Example values.yaml configuration:

   ```yaml
   imageRegistry: "ghcr.io/doublewordai/bit-harbor"
   
   images:
     - name: "gemma-3-4b-it"
       enabled: true
       nodeSelector:
         node-type: "gpu"
       tolerations:
         - key: "gpu"
           operator: "Equal"
           value: "true"
           effect: "NoSchedule"
   ```

9. To monitor image pre-pulling across your cluster:

   kubectl get pods -l app.kubernetes.io/component=image-prepuller --field-selector=status.phase=Running

For more information, visit: https://github.com/doublewordai/bit-harbor
